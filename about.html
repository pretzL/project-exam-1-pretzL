About the author, their profile image and their personal description

Eric Pretzinger | Food For Thought

Food is my hobby and I want to ignite this spark and share my joy with you. As an American growing up in Norway I have been able to enjoy both - the very natural and simple cooking that is characteristic for the Scandinavian countries, as well as the rich, flavorful and pampering cesspool/mixing pot that is the American cuisine. I will be sharing tried and tested recipes, family favorites and simple dishes mostly, but with my own twist. I hope you will enjoy and join me on my culinary journey!
